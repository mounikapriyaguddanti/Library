About Us

The Library has a wide range of books for personality development and competitive examinations. The ambience & well-designed structure is fully automated by NewGenLib software and is utilised by more than 200 readers daily. In addition, the Library offers internet, printing and photocopying services to the readers. Also offer membership for their visitors. The library provide hands-on experience and impart technical skills to the needy. The main vision is to propagate knowledge and create social awareness. The libraryalso help many research scholars to complete their projects and theses.The Library maintains a zero-noise environment to provide a disturbance-free reading experience to the readers as well as visitors. Library has a vast collection of books, including historical fiction, drama poetry, cartoon characters, science fiction, and many more. The place has an air-conditioned seminar hall for accompanying academic programs, cultural events and other meetings ,tranquil reading has amenities like RO drinking water, a wi-fi facility and a parking space. The library will be the perfect place for bookworms.The library offers Telugu and English dailies to the public.
